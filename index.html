Description

Emerging from the techno underground, Arcathrax has been crafting his distinctive sound for the past three years, rapidly garnering hundreds of thousands of streams. Known for his seamless blend of highly synthetic sounds with deep, analog basslines and drum patterns, Arcathrax has carved a niche in the electronic music scene.

Drawing inspiration from the iconic music of the 80s and profoundly influenced by Daft Punk’s Tron album, Arcathrax channels a nostalgic yet futuristic energy into his music, making each track a journey.
