About Me

Welcome to the world of Arcathrax, where cutting-edge techno meets nostalgic 80s vibes. As a dedicated music producer for the past three years, I've had the pleasure of creating sounds that resonate with listeners worldwide. My journey has been marked by hundreds of thousands of streams.

My breakout track, "ВИРТУАЛЬНАЯ ЛЮБОВЬ," a remix of Tanin Jazz, has been a highlight of my career, capturing the essence of my unique style and earning acclaim across the electronic music community. Inspired by the iconic music of the 80s and the futuristic tones of Daft Punk’s Tron album, my music is a fusion of the past and the future, creating a sonic experience that is both nostalgic and forward-thinking.

Beyond producing music, I’m passionate about helping others on their musical journey. Whether it's through professional mixing and mastering services, personalized FL Studio lessons, or crafting custom beats in any genre, I aim to share my knowledge and expertise with aspiring producers and musicians.

Join me on this musical adventure and explore the depths of techno with Arcathrax. Let’s create something extraordinary together.
